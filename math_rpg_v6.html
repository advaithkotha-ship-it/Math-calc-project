Problem Setup:
A colony of bacteria grows at a rate of dP/dt = 14P(1 - P/800), P(0) = 50, where P is the population in thousands and t is time in hours.

A single cell's position is tracked parametrically by x(t) = t - sin(t), y(t) = 1 - cos(t).

A sensor scans the colony in the polar path of r = 3sin(2θ), θ ≥ 0.

The colony emits a signal modeled by the series f(x) = Σ(n=0 to ∞) (-1)ⁿ x^(2n+1) / ((2n+1)·4ⁿ).

ROUND TO THE NEAREST HUNDREDTH FOR ALL PARTS (except Part D — round to nearest thousandth)

Find the particular solution of P, then find lim(x→0⁺) [P(t) - 50] / t.
Your final answer should be P(2) - the limit.

How to Play

Everything you need to survive the dungeon

🗺️ The Run
⚔️Fight through 15 stages of dungeon encounters. Defeat normal mobs on stages 1–4, 6–9, 11–14, face a Knight Captain miniboss on stages 5 and 10, and battle the King on stage 15 to win.
🎯After each fight you visit a rest camp to heal, allocate stat points, craft gear, and choose an event.
📈Enemies scale in difficulty each stage — come prepared!
🔢 Math Combat
❓Every action — attack, heal, or buff — requires you to correctly answer 3 math questions in a row.
✅Answer all 3 correctly and your move fires. A wrong answer resets your progress for that question.
📐Questions include addition, subtraction, and multiplication. Difficulty scales with your stage number.
💡 The player always attacks first each round, then all surviving enemies retaliate. Plan accordingly!
🧙 Classes
⚔️WarriorHigh HP. Scales STR (4% dmg/pt) and HP stat (6% MaxHP + bonus dmg/pt). Enrage for burst damage.
🔮MagePowerful INT scaling (4% dmg/pt). Glass cannon with AoE spells and Mana Overflow burst.
✨PriestFTH scales healing (4%/pt). Sustain through faith. HP stat also boosts heals.
🛡️KnightWarrior evolution. Extra base HP, Order Up buff grants damage and defense simultaneously.
💜WizardMage evolution. Lightning Strike hits all enemies. Mana Overdrive burns targets permanently.
🌿DruidPriest evolution. Nature's Wrath deals 9 dmg + heals 50% HP, Vine Leech steals HP, Nature's Power heals 50%.
💡 Classes can evolve at a rest camp if you meet the requirements. Evolutions keep your moves and add new powerful abilities.
📊 Stats & Scaling
❤️HP stat — increases your max HP. Warrior/Knight: +6%/pt · Priest/Druid: +4%/pt · Mage/Wizard: +3%/pt
⚔️Strength — primary damage stat for Warrior/Knight. +4% dmg/pt. Weak for other classes.
🔮Intelligence — primary damage stat for Mage/Wizard. +4% dmg/pt. Weak for other classes.
✨Faith — primary heal stat for Priest/Druid. +4% healing/pt. Weak for other classes.
💡 There are no stat caps! Invest heavily in your class's primary stat for exponential power growth.
💡 Warriors benefit from stacking HP — it boosts both max HP and deals bonus damage.
🎪 Events
🏪Shop — spend gold on potions and useful items.
💪Training — gain a free stat point in HP or your class's primary stat.
🎲Random Event — mysterious happenings. Could be treasure, curses, or surprises.
🔧Crafting — use materials dropped by enemies to forge armor and weapons.
⚗️ Statuses & Items
🔥Burn — deals damage each turn. Applied by Mage fire spells.
☠️Poison — deals damage each turn. Applied by Priest's Poison Powder.
🩸Bleed — deals damage each turn. Applied by Warrior's Stab.
🧪Healing potions and damage potions can be used during combat at the cost of your turn.
🐀Enemies drop crafting materials: Rat Skins, Leather, Cloth, Iron Ingots. Goblins can steal your gold!
🛡️ Bosses
🛡️Knight Captain (Stage 5 & 10) — 30 HP miniboss. Hits hard. Defeating him grants 3 bonus stat points.
⚔️Prince (Stage 8) — mid-run boss. Defeat him to earn a powerful class-specific weapon upgrade.
👑The King (Stage 15) — 75 HP final boss. Victory ends the run. You win!
